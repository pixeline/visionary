Visionary is a research project aiming to improve web
accessibility for people with visual impairments,
focusing on colour-blind people.

What is colour blindness?

Colour-blindness is the inability to distinguish between certain colours. This condition results from an absence of colour-sensitive pigments in the cone cells of the retina, the nerve layer at the back of the eye.

About colour blindness

Am i colour blind?

Find out if you are colour blind with our free and easy online test. The test only takes a minute and reveals if and how much you are colour-blind.

(Don’t be scared: 1 out of 12 people have some level of colour-blindness).
